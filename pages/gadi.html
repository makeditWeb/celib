Amenities at Célib Gadi

Community Lounge
A versatile space for both work and socializing, featuring a coffee machine, plush sofas, and various tables—perfect for productivity or relaxation.
Relaxation Zone
Unwind, read, or chat in a cozy space with plenty of seating. Next door, the communal dining area invites you to gather and share meals.
Fitness Studio
Work out with cardio and strength equipment, from treadmills to weight machines. A well-equipped space for a balanced fitness routine.
Cinema Room
Enjoy immersive movie nights with a projector and premium visuals. Perfect for private viewing or community screenings.
Shared Kitchen
Equipped with a microwave, cooktop, and sink, the shared kitchen provides a convenient space for cooking and dining together.
Entertainment Area
Have fun with a variety of games in a cozy space with plush sofas, a coffee table, and gaming consoles—perfect for gatherings with friends.
Communal Dining Area
A welcoming space to share meals, enjoy delivery food, and connect with others over dining tables designed for community gatherings.
Social Lounge
Ideal for business meetings and group discussions, featuring a large monitor and meeting tables for a productive setting.
Outdoor Lounge
A relaxing outdoor space with cozy seating and vibrant yellow umbrellas, ideal for socializing or enjoying a quiet moment in nature.
Sky Deck
Take in breathtaking city views from this rooftop escape, a perfect place to relax, socialize, and admire the Seoul skyline.

Shopping & Leisure

Time Square Mall

A short 15-min trip leads to one of Seoul’s largest shopping complexes, packed with global brands, restaurants, and entertainment

IFC Mall & Hangang Park

In 20 mins, enjoy upscale shopping, scenic river walks, and lively outdoor activities in Seoul’s financial hub

Mario Outlet & Hyundai Outlet

Just 5 mins away, these outlet malls offer a wide range of fashion, lifestyle, and home goods at great prices

The Hyundai Seoul

In 20 mins, visit the largest department store in Seoul, featuring luxury shopping, dining, and cultural spaces

Iconic Landmarks

N Seoul Tower (Namsan Tower)

A 30-min journey takes you to Seoul’s most famous observatory, offering breathtaking skyline views

Gangnam District

Within 25 mins, experience high-end shopping, trendy cafés, and Seoul’s dynamic nightlife

Jongno & Insadong

In just 30 mins, step into Seoul’s historic heart, filled with palaces, traditional teahouses, and cultural streets

Sangam World Cup Stadium

In 25mins, experience football matches, major concerts, and large-scale events at one of Seoul’s iconic venues

Culture & Nightlife

Myeongdong

Just 30 mins away, perfect for shopping and trying famous street food

Hongdae

A 35-min trip brings you to Seoul’s hub for indie music, street art, and nightlife

Itaewon

In just 25 mins, explore diverse international cuisine and a dynamic social scene

Beyond Seoul

Reach key areas in Gyeonggi and Incheon in 30mins, making business and travel more accessible
